Tranquil Brindavan

Nestled in the serene landscapes of Chamarajanagar, Tranquil Brindavan is more than just a plotted layout – it’s an invitation to build your dream home in nature’s lap, with all the conveniences of urban living.


Why Chamarajanagar?

Chamarajanagar is no longer just a serene district near Mysuru – it’s rapidly transforming into an investment-worthy zone for plotted layouts, eco-farm ventures, and retirement communities. Proximity to Mysuru (55 km) & Ooty (100 km). Excellent connectivity via NH-948 & upcoming rail projects Government-backed industrial corridors Affordable land pricing with long-term appreciation potential
